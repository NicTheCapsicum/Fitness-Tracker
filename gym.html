Phase guide
Phase 1 — Weeks 1–3 ▾

Rebuild technique, consistency, and baseline fitness. Push-ups: +1 rep/session. Overhead press: 6 kg from Week 2. Goblet squat: 12 kg from Week 3. Row intervals: hold pace with less strain. Swimming: keep paces, focus on smoothness.

Phase 2 — Weeks 4–6 ▾

Increase strength, improve aerobic pace, build power. Push-ups to 3×10/12, then elevated. OH press 6 kg → 8 kg. Goblet 14–18 kg. RDL 12.5–14 kg/hand. Step-ups 5–8 kg. Row Wednesday: 6×5 min @ 2:08–2:10, SR 27–29. Friday: drop splits ~3 sec.

Phase 3 — Weeks 7–9 ▾

Mid-level strength, race-style rowing pace, swim efficiency. Push-ups 3×12–15, add vest in Wk 9. OH press 8 kg 3×8/10. Goblet 20–24 kg. RDL 16–20 kg/hand. Wednesday row: 5×5 min @ 2:06–2:08, SR 28–30. Swim speed: 1:45–1:50.

Phase 4 — Weeks 10–12 ▾

Consolidate strength, peak aerobic, add power. Weighted push-ups 5–8 kg. OH press 10 kg. Goblet 24–28 kg. RDL 20–24 kg. Row Wednesday: 4×6 min @ 2:03–2:06, SR 28–31. Swim: 1:40–1:45 fast, 2:05–2:10 secondary.

Weeks 13–21 ▾

Wks 13–15 Sharpen: Row Monday 10×1km @ 2:05–2:10 SR26. Wed 6×5min @ 2:02–2:06 SR28. Goblet 20kg, RDL 16kg, step-ups 8kg.

Wks 16–18 Volume boost: Monday 12 reps, 7×5min @ 1:58–2:03. Goblet 22–24kg, RDL 20kg.

Wks 19–21 Power: Row 10×1km @ 1:58–2:03 SR28. 6×6min @ 1:55–2:00. OH press 14–16kg, goblet 28–30kg.

Weeks 22–24 — Peak ▾

Highest intensity of 24-week block. Monday 10×1km @ 1:54–1:59 SR28–30. Wednesday 8×4min @ 1:52–1:57 SR30. OH press 16–18kg, goblet 30–32kg, RDL 26kg. Swim: 20×1 length @ 1:30–1:40, secondary 1:50–1:55.
